Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What file formats does RetroReader support?
A: RetroReader currently supports text files (.txt) and PDF files. PDFs are automatically converted to text for the best sentence-by-sentence reading experience.
Q: Is my reading data stored online?
A: No! All your documents, bookmarks, and reading progress are stored locally on your device. I don't collect or store your personal reading data.
Q: Can I backup my bookmarks?
A: Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. I recommend not deleting the app if you want to keep your bookmarks. Cloud backup features may be added in future updates.
Q: How do I share quote images?
A: When reading, bookmark any sentence you like, then use the share feature to generate a beautiful retro-styled quote image that you can share on social media or save to your photos.
Q: Is RetroReader available for both iOS and Android?
A: Yes! RetroReader is available on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.
